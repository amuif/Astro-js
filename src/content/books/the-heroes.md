### A Review of The Heroes

The narrative starts with a sentence that immediately regrets being written. By the second paragraph, the punctuation has declared independence, and the adjectives are on strike.

It is a masterpiece of unintentional architecture.

---

### Plot Highlights

- A staircase that only leads to yesterday.
- A character who is technically a semicolon.
- A subplot involving a very dramatic piece of celery.

The ending is a polite request for more jam.

Overall rating: **7 out of 10 bewildered bookcases**.
